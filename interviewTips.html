  • Always research the role you are applying to. You are likely to be asked why you are applying for the specific role. Think about what your roles and responsibilities will be. What skills will be required to perform well in the role?
  • To convince the interviewer you are the right person for the role you need to convince yourself first!
  • Always research the organisation you are applying to. You are likely to be asked why you want to work for the organisation.
  • What are the organisations objectives/values and how do you meet these?
  • Arrive on time. Arriving late will give a poor first impression.
  • Make sure you are enthusiastic to show your confidence.
  • Remember not to lie.
  • One of the biggest mistakes to make is being critical of others especially former employers/colleagues.
  • Whether successful or not always write down the questions you were asked for future interviews.
